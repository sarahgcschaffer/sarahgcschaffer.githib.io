Hy-Vee Dietitian Webpage

During my internship with Hy-Vee, I worked closely with the dietitians and realized their website was in desperate need of a tune-up.

In under 48 hours, I put together a draft with HTML and CSS that would do what it could in their outdated system. I focused on condensing information, making the top banner more inviting, and using more teal to adhere to the Hy-Vee dietitian brand standards.

ADVRT 336: Iowa DOT and Bucket Media

The following are all part of a campaign my team created for the Iowa DOT with Bucket Media advertising agency. The idea of the campaign was to encourage safe driving and disuade people from texting and driving.

Most of the campaign was done in under three weeks, so a lot of the creative is conceptual and unpolished due to time constraints. My team competed against all the other teams in the class and won the spot of best campign.

ADVRT 434: Mountain Dew Energy

These pieces are part of a campaign still in development. Mountain Dew Energy, previously Mountain Dew Rise before some legal issues, is looking for new digital media ideas from out class.

My team, which I lead, is still working on refining all aspects of our campaign and conducting a second round of primary research. I made the first two pieces in Photoshop and the last one in Canva.
